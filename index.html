About McDonald's

Since 1955, McDonald's has been serving smiles with our iconic burgers, fries, and more. We are the world's leading global foodservice retailer with over 38,000 locations across more than 100 countries. Our mission is simple: deliver delicious food quickly and conveniently while maintaining top quality and outstanding customer service.

Our Vision

To be the favorite place and way for people to enjoy their meals by combining innovation, affordability, and unmatched service.

Our Values

  • Quality & Freshness
  • Speed & Convenience
  • Integrity in Every Bite
  • Commitment to Communities

Sustainability
